Go Chicken

Go Chicken is a little simple game based on simple mechanics, you have only 4 buttons and your skill !
Your goal is to reach the end of each levels by collecting the eggs (or not....)
But wait, it's not that easy... the game is populated with monsters that can kill you instantly, so watch your steps !

The game is available in English and French languages !
It represents 10 months of development, and it's the first game project I've ever completed.

The game received a major update on September 19th 2022, the changelog can be found here.
