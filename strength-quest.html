Strengh Quest

Strength Quest is a small roguelite card game made for the BlackThordProd Game Jam #3.
The theme was less is more. I've always wanted to make and card game and it was the perfect time to do it.
To fit the theme I've implemented a strength multiplier : the more health you have the less damage you deal.
You have to find the right balance between health and damage, don't be to greedy !

My entry to the game jam was placed 2th in the Theme Category !
