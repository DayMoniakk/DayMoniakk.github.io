Neuro-Sama Mod

This mod aims to bring the universe of the AI vtuber Neuro-sama to the game Brotato.
It was my first game modding experience, it was really refreshing to work on something new!

The mod contains:
● 6 characters
● 3 weapons
● 25 items
● custom main menu
● custom musics
● custom hurt sounds for the mod's characters

Supported languages:
● English
● Chinese (fan translation)

More informations on the Steam Workshop page.

Project Status:

Released (version 1.1.1)

Technology:

Godot 3.5 - GDscript
