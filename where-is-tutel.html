Where is tutel?

Where is tutel is a mix between "Where's waldo" and "Wanted" minigame from Super Mario 64 DS.

In this game you have to find the turtle (Vedal987) on the screen among all the clones of Neuro, Evil Neuro, Hiyori and their drones.
Map, props and characters are randomly generated. The difficulty will randomly increase the further you go.

The game was made in 72 hours for the Neuro-sama Birthday Game Jam, go check out Neuro-sama and her creator Vedal987, they are amazing !
The theme was "Lost and found", usually I don't enter in game jams with a short deadline but sometimes you have to move outside of your comfort zone.

For this jam I choosed to use the Godot engine instead of using Unity as usual, this was a strategic choice that helped me to quickly deliver a polished product on my own instead of being slowed by the speed of the Unity editor.

My entry was placed 2th in the Theme category and 9th in the Overall Category !
