Guncraft Duels

Guncraft Duels is a minigame originally created by Nathan818 & Commando34 for the Minecraft server Ascentia in 2011.

This is a standalone miniaturized version of the Guncraft remake released in 2021 by my friend Nosfear and myself for our server that closed on October 30 2022.

This mini-game has been completely remade from scratch (with the exception of the weapons code, which did not require rewriting)

The goal of this version is to offer to players the possibility of playing again this mythical game with friends and without the fuss.
