Guncraft Duels

Guncraft Duels is a minigame originally created by Nathan818 & Commando34 for the Minecraft server Ascentia in 2011.
This is a standalone miniaturized version of the Guncraft remake released in 2021 by me & Nosfear for our server that closed on October 30 2022.
This mini-game has been completely redone from A to Z (apart from the weapon code that have been re-used)

The goal of this version is to offer to players the possibility of playing again this mythical game with friends and without the fuss.
