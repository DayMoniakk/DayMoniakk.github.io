Five Nights At Nan's

Five Nights At Nan's is a fan game I built for my friend streamer Nan7s_Peluche who is currently streaming on Twitch !
It is heavily inspired by the horror franchise Five Nights at Freddy's.

The game was build in 2 months with the Godot Engine.
The lighting is completely realtime based which allows me to turn off every single lights during gameplay.

I built this project to find out if the 3D part of the engine suits my needs for future projects but the engine tends to become slower every time the project files grows in size.
The Godot engine is still a very promising piece of software, I can't wait to see its evolution !
